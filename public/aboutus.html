About Algo-Rhythm


Algo-Rhythm is a music discovery app designed to help users find songs, artists, and recommendations that match their taste. Instead of endlessly searching for something new, users can browse trending music, explore similar songs and artists, and save favorites in a more interactive and personalized way.

Discover

Browse trending artists and songs to quickly see what is popular and worth listening to.

Explore

Personalize

Save favorites and build a more custom music experience through your profile and recommendation pages.
